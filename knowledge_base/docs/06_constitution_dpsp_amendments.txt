=== DIRECTIVE PRINCIPLES OF STATE POLICY (Part IV, Art.36-51) ===

DPSP are not justiciable (cannot be enforced by courts) but are fundamental in governance.

Art.38: Secure social order for welfare.
Art.39: Adequate livelihood, equal pay for equal work, no concentration of wealth.
Art.39A: Free legal aid — Legal Services Authorities Act 1987.
Art.40: Panchayati Raj — 73rd Amendment 1992.
Art.41: Right to work, education, public assistance.
Art.42: Maternity relief and humane work conditions.
Art.44: Uniform Civil Code — currently in force only in Goa.
Art.45: Early childhood care till age 6.
Art.48A: Protect and improve environment (42nd Amendment) — basis for environmental laws.
Art.50: Separation of judiciary from executive.
Art.51: International peace and respect for international law.

DPSP vs Fundamental Rights:
Champakam Dorairajan 1951: FR prevailed over DPSP.
Kesavananda Bharati 1973: Basic Structure doctrine — harmony between FR and DPSP.
Minerva Mills 1980: Balance between FR and DPSP is part of basic structure.

=== FUNDAMENTAL DUTIES (Art.51A, Part IVA) ===

Added by 42nd Amendment 1976. 11 duties — not justiciable but courts use them to uphold laws.
(a) Abide by Constitution (b) Cherish national struggle ideals (c) Uphold sovereignty
(d) Defend country (e) Promote harmony (f) Value composite culture (g) Protect environment
(h) Develop scientific temper (i) Safeguard public property (j) Strive for excellence
(k) Provide education to children 6-14 years (86th Amendment)

=== KEY AMENDMENTS ===

1st (1951): 9th Schedule added.
42nd (1976): Added Fundamental Duties; Preamble amended (Socialist, Secular, Integrity).
44th (1978): Restored Art.19, 20, 21; removed right to property from FRs.
52nd (1985): Anti-defection — 10th Schedule.
61st (1988): Voting age 21→18.
73rd/74th (1992): Mandatory Panchayati Raj and Urban Local Bodies elections.
86th (2002): Art.21A free education; Art.51A(k) added.
101st (2016): GST — Art.246A.
103rd (2019): 10% EWS reservation Art.15(6), Art.16(6).
105th (2021): States/UTs restored power to identify OBCs.
