=== EMPLOYEES' PROVIDENT FUNDS ACT, 1952 ===

Coverage (S.1): Establishments with 20 or more employees. Once covered, always covered.
Three Schemes: EPF Scheme 1952 (retirement savings), EPS 1995 (pension), EDLI 1976 (life insurance).
Contribution rates:
  Employee: 12% of basic wages + DA + retaining allowance.
  Employer: 3.67% to EPF, 8.33% to EPS (capped at ₹15,000 basis), 0.5% to EDLI, 0.5% EPF admin.
Wage ceiling: ₹15,000/month (can voluntarily contribute above).
UAN: Universal Account Number — portable across employers; seeded with Aadhaar.

Withdrawal:
Full withdrawal: retirement at 58, permanent disability, leaving India, 2+ months unemployment.
Partial withdrawal: housing (after 5 yrs), marriage (after 7 yrs, 50% own share), education, medical treatment.

Forms: Form 19 (EPF), Form 10C (EPS), Form 31 (partial withdrawal).
Online claims via EPFO portal (epfindia.gov.in) — processed in 3 days (online) to 20 days (manual).

=== PAYMENT OF GRATUITY ACT, 1972 ===

Coverage: Factories, mines, plantations, shops, educational institutions with 10+ employees.
Eligibility (S.4(1)): 5 years continuous service on superannuation, retirement, resignation, or termination.
5-year condition WAIVED for death or disablement.

Calculation (S.4(2)):
Gratuity = Last drawn Basic + DA × 15/26 × Completed years of service
(15 days wages per completed year; 26 working days per month)
Fractions: 6+ months rounds up to 1 year; less than 6 months ignored.
Maximum: ₹20 lakhs (Notification 2018). Government employees have no ceiling.

Example: Basic+DA ₹50,000/month, 9 years service → ₹50,000 × 15/26 × 9 = ₹2,59,615

Forfeiture (S.4(6)): Wholly for conviction involving moral turpitude. Partially for willful damage to property.
Employer must pay within 30 days of determination. Delay: simple interest at bank rate.
Non-payment: criminal offence — 6 months to 2 years imprisonment + fine.

OTHER LAWS:
Maternity Benefit Act 1961 (amended 2017): 26 weeks paid leave (first 2 children); 12 weeks for third child.
Minimum Wages Act 1948: State-specific; revision every 5 years; non-payment is criminal offence.
Payment of Bonus Act 1965: Minimum 8.33%, maximum 20% of salary; payable within 8 months of FY end.

PRACTICAL NOTES:
Q: What is the EPF contribution rate? A: Employee 12%, Employer split 3.67% EPF + 8.33% EPS.
Q: When am I eligible for gratuity? A: After 5 years continuous service.
Q: What is the maximum gratuity? A: ₹20 lakhs for private sector employees.
Q: How is gratuity calculated? A: Last Basic+DA × 15/26 × years of service.
Q: How many weeks maternity leave? A: 26 weeks for first two children.
